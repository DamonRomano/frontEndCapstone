You walk down the dusty path from your home near the edge of the village. 'Fore long, the path begins to trace the edge of a yellow wood. About a quarater-mile from home now and the path diverges; one road into the wood, th'other wraps back around to the crazy old milkman's place.

Taking the road less traveled-by, you follow as it bends into the undergrowth.

You come upon the Home for Old Crones, a dilapidated neo-Colonial number with stereotypical flaking white paint and an even-more-stereotypical sense of foreboding.

One particular crone separates herself from the...what?... Gathering? Throng? Flock? What to they call multiple crones?

Ah! MURDER!

A murder of crones.

Crone Prime looks about ready to speak to you, but pauses. She looks with conspiratorial glee to her left...to her right. And then she lasciviously opens her housecoat.

"Hey, Stranger! Whadda ya buyin'?"
